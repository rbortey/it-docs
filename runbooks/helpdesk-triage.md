# Helpdesk Triage Runbook

## Intake Priorities

1. Business outage or customer-impacting issue.
2. Identity or MFA issue affecting a named user.
3. Vendor support window or implementation blocker.
4. Routine access request.

## MFA Prompts

Repeated MFA prompts should be treated carefully.

- Confirm the user initiated the sign-in.
- Confirm the application involved.
- Confirm approximate time and source location if available.
- Do not approve unexpected prompts just to clear the ticket.
- If the user confirms they are actively signing in and the request matches the support window, document the approval reason.

## Evidence To Capture

- Reporter name and email.
- System involved.
- Timestamp and approximate duration.
- Screenshots or error messages when available.
- Any vendor or support contact involved.
